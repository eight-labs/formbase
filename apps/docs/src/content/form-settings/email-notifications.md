# Email Notifications

Formbase can send an email every time a new submission arrives.

## Example 1: Enable notifications

1. Open your form settings.
2. Toggle **Email notifications** on.
3. Save changes.


## Example 2: Notifications for critical forms

Enable notifications on forms that represent leads or support requests.

## Tips

- Use a shared inbox for teams.
- Combine notifications with a CRM using exports.
